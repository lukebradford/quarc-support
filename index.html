About

QUARC reimagines the classic falling-block puzzle with beautifully curved pieces. Every shape is built from concave and convex arcs — rotate, drop, and clear rows to climb the leaderboard.

How to Play

  • Tap left or right to move a piece
  • Tap the center to rotate
  • Swipe down to drop
  • Match curves to complete rows and clear them

FAQ

The game feels too fast / too slow.
QUARC starts at a gentle pace and gradually accelerates. Try to clear rows early to keep the board manageable.

What are the golden shapes?
When two concave curves meet and form a sealed gap, a golden gem fills the space. These count toward completing a row.

What happens when curves don't match?
If two convex curves collide, the piece becomes a "goner" and disappears — it won't help you clear the row.
